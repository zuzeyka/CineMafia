HEROES
1.
Hawkeye - On the first night, sees three characters but doesn’t know who is who.
2.
Black Mamba - When shot for the first time, reveals their card and chooses who leaves the game instead of them.
3.
Rick - Can cancel night events once per game.
4.
Mentalist - Each night shows the numbers of two players. At the end of the round, the first sees the role card of the second.
5.
Neo - Each night chooses invulnerability or a vote for three. Invulnerability doesn’t work against Kira.
6.
Professor Xavier - Plays with open eyes during the villains’ phase on the fourth night.
7.
Blade - Can shoot every night. If killed, the shot misses.
8.
Wolverine - Cannot be killed by villains.
9.
Lady Melisandre - Can bring back an eliminated player once per game.
10.
Doctor House - Can three times per game protect another player from being killed in the current round.
11.
Mr. Robot - Shows the numbers of two players and learns if they are on the same team.
12.
Naruto - Reveals their role card and becomes immortal when five votes are cast against them.
13.
Mad Hatter - Each night chooses a red role not in the game and uses its ability.
14.
Robocop - Twice per game, can choose a player who reveals their role card in the morning.
15.
Iron Man - Once per game, can choose two players at night and leave the game with them.
NEUTRALS
16.
Joker - Wins if voted out during the game.
17.
Saw - Wins if they are in the final four (or three in games with 10 players).
18.
Frank Underwood - Wins if they vote in the majority three times (twice in games with 10 players).
19.
Sherlock Holmes - Wins if they name the roles of all players. Each night, learns the role of one player.
20.
Kenny - Wins if they guess who killed them. Each night, learns if a player can kill.
21.
Saul Goodman - Wins if voted for twice and survives both votes.
VILLAINS
22.
Darth Vader - Cannot be killed by Blade.
23.
Thanos - Once per game, can cancel a vote.
24.
Sub-Zero - Blocks one player’s ability each night for the round.
25.
Shang Tsung - At the start of the game, replaces their role card with another. Does not copy abilities.
26.
Lord Voldemort - Kills two players per night if they are the last villain standing.
--
Kira - Each night, guesses a player’s role. If correct, the shot lands.
--
Freddy Krueger - Starts waking up and killing after being eliminated.
--
Tyler Durden - Shows two numbers at night. If the first votes for the second, they both die.
--
Samara Morgan - On the first night, marks three players who die one by one until Samara is eliminated.
